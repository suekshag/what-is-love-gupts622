DAY 1

LOVE EXPRESSIONS IN INDIA

I love you- Mei tumsey pyaar kartaa/ karti (female) hu!

My passion burns like a flame.- मेरा जुनून एक लौ की तरह जलता है.

I love you with all my heart and life- Maein dilo jaan say tumhe chahtaa hoon

You look beautiful-तुम बहुत सुन्दर हो

You’re my life, my breath, and my heartbeat. You’re my everything.- App meri jindagi, meri dhadkane aur meri saason main hain….aap hi mere sab kuch hain ji

You are the most beautiful woman in the world- आप दुनिया में सबसे सुंदर महिला हैं.

My heart is completely infatuated/charmed by you- Meraa dil pooree tarah tumhaaare ooper fidaa hai

You have a beautiful name- आपका नाम सुन्दर है

I love you with every beat of my heart- Hum tumhay dilkeh her dharcun say prem kertah hoon

My heart speaks the language of love- मेरा दिल प्यार की बात कर रहा है
